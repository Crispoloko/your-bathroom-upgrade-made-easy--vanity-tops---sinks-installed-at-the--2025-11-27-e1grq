About Bathroom Pro

Your trusted partner in bathroom renovations for over a decade

Founded in 2012, Bathroom Pro started with a simple mission: to make premium bathroom upgrades accessible to homeowners of all budgets. What began as a small family operation has grown into one of the region's most trusted bathroom renovation specialists. Over the past 12 years, we've completed thousands of successful projects, from modest vanity upgrades to complete bathroom transformations. Our reputation is built on honest pricing, exceptional craftsmanship, and unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction.

Today, we're dedicated to revolutionizing how people approach bathroom renovations. We believe that everyone deserves a beautiful, functional bathroom regardless of budget constraints. Our core values of integrity, quality, and innovation drive everything we do. We invest in the latest installation techniques, maintain rigorous quality standards, and continuously train our team to deliver excellence. Our vision is to be the most trusted bathroom renovation partner in the region, known for transforming spaces and exceeding expectations on every single project.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our bathroom upgrade services

Most bathroom vanity and sink installations are completed within 1-3 days, depending on the complexity of your project. For standard single-sink installations, we typically complete the work in just one day. Our team works efficiently while maintaining the highest quality standards. We'll provide you with a specific timeline during your free in-home consultation so you can plan accordingly.

We stand behind our work with a comprehensive 5-year workmanship warranty on all installations. This covers any issues related to installation quality, including plumbing connections, sealing, and structural integrity. Additionally, all materials come with manufacturer warranties ranging from 1-10 years depending on the product. We also offer optional extended warranty plans for added peace of mind.

Remnants are excess inventory from large-scale projects and overstock from manufacturers. These are brand-new, high-quality vanity tops and sinks that didn't sell at retail prices. We partner directly with manufacturers to source these premium products at significant discounts. You get the same quality and durability as full-price items, but at 30-50% less cost. Perfect for homeowners who want luxury finishes without the luxury price tag.
